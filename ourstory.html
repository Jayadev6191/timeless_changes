Timeless Changes Foundation is a nonprofit organization that offers seminars that teach skills such as financial and college planning with a goal of empowering people with a spirit of service.Timeless Changes Foundation’s programs support non-glamorous but essential activities, filling gaps where most other charitable and not-for-profit organizations tend to leave out of their mandate. We choose to find committed individuals and organizations that are engaged in activities in one of our four Focus Areas and support them in ways of their choosing, filling in the gaps of where they are not currently funded.

We believe that when people are empowered through education, self-reflection, and empathy, they can help empower themselves and others to bring about timeless changes in their lives. In order to be empowered, people need to have different levels of needs met. We offer educational services and work with existing partners and programs to support people locally and internationally with different stages on their journey to empowering themselves and others.With your help, we can build kindness.We are a 501c3 Non-Profit recognized by the IRS in the USA as a Charitable organization.



Meet Our Team



Ms. Lakshmi Ayyagari, Ph.D. is the President and CFO of the Foundation. She is passionate about education and financial planning. She has experience in teaching pre-school children to undergraduates in various cultural backgrounds and has expertise in financial planning, college planning, and community development. Her strengths in financial and planning and community building guide the implementation of the programs and growth of the Foundation.

Mr. Prabu Ayyagari, Ph.D. is the Founder and CEO of the Foundation. He is passionate about promoting sustainable development through education and entrepreneurship. With 20 years of experience in business development, project and program management and in science and technology, his strengths contribute to the organization’s administration, vision, and project development.

Mr. Narender Kande, MBA (PGDRM), is a rural management professional with about two decades of experience in managing and guiding large-scale development programs at DHAN Foundation. He is one of the founding members of DHAN Foundation, a renowned development organization in India working in nine states and serving about a million people. He served as advisor for Sir Ratan Tata Trust and other development organizations. He currently works with the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation. His contacts in the world of nonprofit organizations and his experience with microfinance add value to the Foundation.

Ms. Sneha Ayyagari is a student at Stanford University. She is dedicated to promoting harmony between humans, the environment, and animals by empowering communities through education and culturally appropriate solutions. Her experience in non-profit leadership, science and technology research, product design, and education guides the Timeless Changes vision and implementation.

Ms. Raga Ayyagari is a student at Stanford University. She is passionate about the connections between education, health, and environmental issues and is dedicated to empowering others through volunteerism and education. Her insight and experience with social entrepreneurship, community based market research and scientific research, and education projects benefit the Timeless Changes Foundation’s program development and implementation.

Mr. Krishnajee Ayyagari, Ph.D., has experience as a banker and an engineer. His extensive background in the nurturing of small scale industries in developing economies gives unique guidance to the Foundation’s projects.
